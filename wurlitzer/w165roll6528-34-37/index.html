Wurlitzer 165 Band Organ Rolls, Classical

Played using eplay165 with Rich Olsen's Band Organ sound font.

Wurlitzer did not issue much classical music, it was probably not popular with most of their customers. Below is nearly all of it, the rest is on roll 6513. However what there is has been very well arranged for the 165 and is worth a listen.

3. Roll 6528 first issued 1921

  1. Morning, Noon, And Night In Vienna. Overture by Franz von Suppe, February 1844
  2. William Tell Overture by Gioacchino Rossini, 1829

4. Roll 6534A first issued 1923

  1. Bridal Rose Overture Overture by Calixa Lavallée, 1888
  2. Selection from "La Traviata" by Giuseppe Verdi, 1853

5. Roll 6537A first issued 1925

  1. La Norma, Fantasia by Vincenzo Bellini, 1831
  2. Zampa Overture by Louis J. Herold, 1831
