Wurlitzer 150 Band Organ Rolls

2. Roll 14300 (Rolls 13300 and "Polish Roll" 13308 Combined)

Played on a fully instrumented and activated Style 153 Band Organ.

This music was recorded November 2013 and includes the latest information available.

  1. Clarinet Polka. Jagiello, Dec 16, 1913. Adapted from "Dziadunio Polka" by Karol Namyslowski
  2. Sophia Polka.
  3. Beer Barrel Polka. Brown & Voda, Mar 30, 1939. From the 1939 Broadway musical comedy "Yokel Boy"
  4. Hosio Suisia Polka.
  5. Bride And Groom Polka. Roberts & Lee, Jan 12, 1948
  6. One More Waltz. Jimmy McHugh, Jul 11, 1930. From the 1930 MGM motion picture "Love In The Rough"
  7. Metro Polka. Evans & Vaughn, Feb 20, 1951
  8. Let Us Be Gay Polka. Sam H. Stept, Sep 15, 1934
  9. Hop Scotch Polka. Carl Sigman, Gene Rayburn, William Whitlock,1949
  10. Szta Jerek Waltz. Arranged by A. Omelczuk, May 5, 1925
  11. Too Fat Polka. MacLean & Richardson, Aug 25, 1947
  12. Our Kosciuszko March.
