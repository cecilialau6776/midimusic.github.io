Wurlitzer 165 Band Organ Rolls, Marches
Organ: Style 165

2. B.A.B Roll 404

Played using eplay165 with Rich Olsen's Band Organ sound font.

This roll was adapted and recut from a B.A.B 66 key roll by Oswald Wurdeman.
It has limited percussion and only some of the normal Wurlitzer 165 controls.
The snare drum is adapted badly and is sometimes off beat.
The only reason I have included this roll here is because it contains some good music.
  1. Entry Of The Gladiators (Op. 68). Julius Fucik, March 1902.
  2. Under The Double Eagle, Unter den Doppeladler". J. F. Wagner, Jun 1913
  3. The Official West Point March". Philip Egner, Jun 1928.
  4. Golden Gate. Edwin Franko Goldman, Jan 1939.
  5. American Patrol. F. W. Meacham, Mar 1885.
  6. Sabre And Spurs. March Of The American Cavalry, John P. Sousa, Jul 1918.
  7. Junior Order March. William Edward Menghi, Oct 1932.
  8. The Whip. Abe Holzmann, Feb 1913
  9. Stars And Stripes Forever. John P. Sousa, May 1887.
  10. The Army Air Corps. Official song of the United States Army Air Corps, Robert Crawford, Oct 1939.
