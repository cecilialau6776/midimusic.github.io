Wurlitzer 165 Band Organ Rolls

2. Roll 6505

Played on a fully instrumented and activated Style 165 Band Organ.

Note: The first two tunes of this roll are missing and unknown. I have replaced them with tune 1 from roll 6513, Poet and Peasant which is wonderful music beautifully arranged for the Wurlitzer 165 and tune 9 from roll 6526, Entry of the Gladiators. (Which has more shutter action than I have found on any other Wurlitzer roll.)
Tunes 3 to 10 updated 14 January 2015 using the engine of my software Wurlitzer 165 version 1.1.
  1. Poet and Peasant Overture. Franz von Suppe, May 1854.
  2. Entry of the Gladiators (Op.68). Thunder and Blazes. Julius Fucik, Apr 13, 1911. Triumphal March
  3. The Rainbow March. Louis Maurice, Nov 14, 1917. March
  4. Dreaming, Valse. Archibald Joyce, Dec 2, 1911. Waltz
  5. Thurston. Anthony J. Stasny, 1913. March
  6. Cecile Waltz. Frank W. McKee, May 29, 1914. Waltz
  7. Under The Big Tent. Ned Brill, May 1, 1913. March
  8. Dream Days. C. L. Johnson, Jun 5, 1913. Waltz
  9. Spirit Of America (A Patriotic Patrol). J. S. Zamecnik, May 10, 1917. March
  10. Aloha Oe. Princess L. K. Liliuokalani, Nov 14, 1884. Waltz
