Wurlitzer 150 Band Organ Rolls

1. Roll 13173 "Waltz Roll"

Played using eplay150 with Rich Olsen's Band Organ sound font.

  1. The Flower Of Italy. G. de Stefano, Sep 24, 1902
  2. Nights Of Gladness. Charles Ancliffe, Aug 24, 1912
  3. Missouri Waltz. John Valentine Eppel, Oct 10, 1914
  4. Blue Rose. Frederick Knight Logan, Jan 2, 1917
  5. Geraldine. Henry Lodge, Mar 3, 1915
  6. Millicent. Frank W. McKee, Nov 14, 1914
  7. Hawaiian Moonlight. F. Henri Klickmann, Mar 20, 1918
  8. Sphinx. Francis Popy, May 9, 1916
In my opinion many of these tunes are spoiled by over use of the machine gun like reiterating snare drum, particularly when the shutters are closed.
Would it be better to quieten it down by reducing its volume or to stop it from reiterating?
