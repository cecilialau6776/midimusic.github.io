Do You Recognize Any Of This Music?

I do not know the Wurlitzer Roll Number of either of these Style 125 rolls. The tunes are all waltzes.

Please listen to the music below and see if you can recognize (and can put a name to) any of these tunes.
If you can name any of the tunes please contact me at the email address in "About MidiMusic". Your reward will be to have your name associated with the piece you recognize.

I will keep this page updated with any progress. I will also continue the search myself, extending my search to w150 candidate rolls.

Unknown Roll w93, Audio
