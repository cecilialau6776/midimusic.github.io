Recognize Music

Do You Recognize Any Of This Music?

Please listen to the music below and see if you can recognize (and can put a name to) any of these tunes, they are all waltzes. Also listen to see if you agree with the naming of those which have been identified.

If you can name any of the tunes or disagree with the identified tunes please contact me at the email address in "About MidiMusic". Your reward will be to have your name associated with the piece you recognize.

I will keep this page updated with any progress. I will also continue the search myself.

1.0 Unknown Roll w93, Audio

We now know that this roll is uncatalogued and there is no matching w150 roll. It is likely to be roll 3092 as this is the only 1923 or later uncatalogued roll until roll 3136 in 1928. The next steps are to identify each tune. It will be most productive to look at w150 waltzes October 1923 and earlier which have NOT been published on w125 rolls.

2.0 Unknown Roll w82, Audio

This roll may be w125 roll 3096, 3102 or another all waltz roll of the same era; however bear in mind that the lost tunes may not be waltzes.

It could also be an uncatalogued roll but only if it is prior to 1917 or later than 1928, unless it is roll 3092, now unlikely; see above. If it is uncatalogued there may be a similar w150 roll which will help to identify the music.
