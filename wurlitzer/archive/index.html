Wurlitzer Band Organ e-Roll Archive

This is an archive of Wurlitzer Band Organ Music Rolls in e-Roll format.
These e-rolls contain all of their original controls.

I have produced two players, eplay and eplayWin32, which can play these e-rolls.
The best way to play these rolls (as of February 2018) is to use eplayWin32 feeding its output to eplayOrgan. Other players have not been updated recently.

Download e-roll players, eplay or eplayWin32 These directly play Wurlitzer and Estey e-rolls. EplayWin32 is a fully graphical program which plays e-rolls via my eplayOrgan program. Eplay runs on Windows and iMac, eplayWin32 and eplayOrgan run on Windows and under Wine on iMac and most Linux systems.

Both players use Rich Olsen's sound fonts, widely acknowledged to be the best.
See also Music To My Ears The Story of Rich Olsen's Band Organ Sound Font.

All the rolls below have now been adjusted so they play at the correct speed when played with eplay or eplayWin32.

w165 e-roll playing error list

All e-rolls in this error list have melody notes being played without any melody stops being set. I suspect most casual listeners would not notice this and in some cases the "errors" were present when the roll was initially issued.
Causes of errors and proposed corrective actions are indicated in the list.

6501-04 ** VERY short duration scanning glitch. Action: delete glitch. Later cancel is clearly intended. Action none.
6513-01 ** Cancel followed by gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
-08 ** Cancel followed by long gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
6514-01 ** Cancel followed by long gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
-04 ** Cancel followed by long gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
-07 ** Cancel followed by gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
-08 ** Cancel followed by gap with nothing, then stops are set. Action: add "Swells off" in gap.
-09 ** Complex problems!! Test again after adding "Swells off" in gaps.
6516-07 ** VERY short duration scanning glitch. Action: delete glitch.
6519-09 ** A bit strange but clearly intended. Action: none.
6526-01 ** Clearly intended. Action: none.
-06 ** VERY short duration scanning glitch. Action: delete glitch.
6554-10 ** Occurs four times and is clearly not an error. Action: none.

NOTE: None of these corrections are yet on line.
