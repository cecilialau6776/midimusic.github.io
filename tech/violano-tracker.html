Violano e-rolls have no midi offset.

Roll

14.25" wide with holes splaced 0.11428"

Tuning

Tuning A = 435Hz, the Factory Standard.

NOTES:
1. Staccato: This is a very brief signal to terminate the current playing violin notes from the start of this signal and for the duration of this signal. (While applied it lifts the bow wheels from the strings).

2. Tremolo: This has to be turned on in short bursts as otherwise it will weaken the paper roll. The latest rolls do not use it at all. Usually it is permanently engaged by users.

3. Violin bowing default: There are 5 levels of bowing pressure 1..5. The default pressure is level 3.

4. Piano Acc and Piano mute: Believed to be brief signals to turn the piano accompanyment on an off. Early Violano's either did not have a piano or it was a seperate attachment. (Still under investigation).

5. Rosin rel: Early Violanos automatically applied rosin on rewind. For later models , rosin was applied only when signalled. (Still under investigation).
