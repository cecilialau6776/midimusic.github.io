MidiMusic Android Apps

These apps are digitally signed, require no "permissions" and will not bombard you with ads.
They are free to download and free to use.

In order to install these .apk's you need to get them into the Downloads directory of your Android phone or tablet. You can either download direct by displaying this page on your phone or tablet or download to a PC or Mac and then transfer the .apk file manually to your phone or tablet.

Please note that these are my first useful android apps and are not yet perfect. I aim to improve and update them as time permits.

The only known bug, which is harmless, occurs when you try to exit the app using the "back" key on your phone or tablet. It leaves a message behind which you have to cancel.

The apps are designed to play only in portrait mode.

Scott Joplin

The Scott Joplin MidiMusicApp will play all of Scott Joplin's piano music. All the music is contained within the app and will play without an Internet connection.
