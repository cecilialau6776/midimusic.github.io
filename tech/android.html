MidiMusic Android Apps

Scott Joplin

This app will play all of Scott Joplin's piano music. All the music is contained within the app and will play without an Internet connection.

The app is digitally signed, requires no "permissions" and will not bombard you with ads.

MidiMusicapp.apk Download from secure site.

In order to install this .apk you need to get it into the Downloads directory of your Android phone or tablet. You can either download it direct by displaying this page on your phone or tablet or download to a PC or Mac and transfer it manually.

Please note that this is my first useful android app and it is not yet perfect.

The only known bug, which is harmless, occurs when you try to exit the app using the "back" key on your phone or tablet. It leaves a message behind which you have to cancel.

This app is designed to play only in portrait mode.

The picture shows it playing on a Nexus 7 tablet.
