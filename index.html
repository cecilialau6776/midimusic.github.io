Introduction

This website contains:-
  1. A playable library of all Scott Joplin's published music and songs.
  2. Playable Videos of Scott Joplin's music.
  3. Sheet music which can be viewed (or followed) as the music plays.
  4. Playable Wurlitzer band organ music and videos. Plus a Wurlitzer e-roll music archive.
  5. Playable Wurlitzer caliola music and videos.
  6. Technical information about midi, music rolls and Wurlitzer
  7. Literature: Computer humour, Kochel Catalog of Mozart's music, Aesop's Fables.
