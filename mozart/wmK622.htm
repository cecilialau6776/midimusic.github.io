  • 11. Mozart, K622 Clarinet Concerto in A major
    • Origin: Vienna, October 1791
    • Scoring: clarinet solo, 2 flutes, 2 bassoons, 2 horns, strings
    • Movements: Allegro. Adagio. Rondo: Allegro.
