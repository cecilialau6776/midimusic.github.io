CSW900 Mozart, K620 Die Zauberflöte (The Magic Flute)

An Opera in 2 Acts

The music on this page may be freely played and distributed for non commercial use.
  • 10. Mozart, K620 Die Zauberflöte (The Magic Flute)
    • Origin: Vienna, begun spring 1791; completed September 28, 1791
    • Author: Emanuel Schikanedir

  • Scene: Egypt in the neighbourhood of a temple of Isis and Osiris.

  • Characters and story.

  • Overture.
  • Act I
  • 1 Introduction. Tamino and Three Ladies: Zu Hülfe! (O help me!).
  • 2 Song. Papageno: Der Vogelfänger bin ich ja (I am the jolly bird-catcher)
  • 3 Aria Tamino: Dies Bildniss ist bezaubernd schön (O loveliness beyond compare).
  • 4 Recitative and Aria. The Queen of Night: O zittre nicht, mein leiber Sohn (Be not afraid, O noble youth).
  • 5 Quintet. Tamino, Papageno, Three Ladies: Hm, hm, hm! Der Arme kann von Strafe sagen ('Tis hard such punishment to suffer).
  • 6 Terzetto. Monostatos, Pamina, Papageno: Du feines Täubchen (My pretty maid).
  • 7 Duet. Pamina, Papageno: Bei Münnern, welche (The kindly voice).
  • 8 Finale. Wie stark ist nicht dein Zauberton (O voice of magic melody).

  • Act II
  • 9 March.
  • 10 Aria. Sarastro: O Isis und Osiris (Oh, hear us, Isis and Osiris!).
  • 11 Duet. Two Priests: Bewahret euch (Beware the wiles).
  • 12 Quintet. Three Ladies, Tamino, Papageno: Wie? wie? wie? (Why oh why).
  • 13 Aria. Monostatos: Alles fühlt der Liebe Freuden (All with passion's fever tingle).
  • 14 Aria. Queen of the Night: Der Hölle Rache kocht (I'll have revenge).
  • 15 Aria. Sarastro: In diesen heil'gen Hallen kennt (We know no thought of vengeance).
  • 16 Terzetto. Three Genii: Seid uns zum zweitenmal (Before the gates).
  • 17 Aria. Pamina: Ach, ich fühl's (Ah, 'tis gone).
  • 18 Chorus. Priests: O Isis und Osiris, welche Wonne! (To Isis and Osiris thanks).
  • 19 Terzetto. Pamina, Tamino, Sarastro: Soll ich dich (And shall I never).
  • 20 Song. Papageno:Ein Mädchen oder Weibchen wünscht ('Tis love, they say).
  • 21 Finale.
