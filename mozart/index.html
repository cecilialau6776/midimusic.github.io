Mozart Music Library

The Mozart music is from one of my old web sites and is still in midi format.
I plan to upgrade the music and add a proper player, similar to the Joplin player.
Meantime the music will play provided you have a browser which can play midi.

  • W.A.Mozart, a brief biography.

A Taste of Mozart's music in MIDI format.
1. K191 Bassoon Concerto 2. K279 Piano Sonata in C major, No.1
3. K299 Flute and Harp Concerto in C major 4. K381 Piano Sonata in D major (four hands)
5. K449 Piano Concerto in E flat major, No. 14 6. K452 Piano and Wind Quintet in E flat major
7. K457 Piano Sonata in C minor, No.14 8. K551 Symphony No.41 in C major - Jupiter
9. K581 Clarinet Quintet in A major 10. K620 The Magic Flute
11. K622 Clarinet Concerto in A major

All MIDI files on this page conform to the General MIDI standard and have been saved in Standard MIDI File format 1, Technical information. They will sound best if played via a General MIDI wavetable based synthesiser followed by a High Fidelity amplifier and speakers. The quality of MIDI sequencing varies from file to file.
If you don't know what this means it does not matter - just play and enjoy.
