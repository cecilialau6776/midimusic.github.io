Speaking for Thorn and EMI Group Pensioners

The Thorn & EMI Group Pensioners' Association is a voluntary organisation which exists to promote and safeguard the interests and benefits of Thorn & EMI Group Pensioners. Anyone who is receiving a Thorn or EMI Group Pension or is entitled to a deferred Thorn or EMI Group Pension can become a member.

Key Objectives for the Association

  • To protect the interest of all Pensioners and Deferred Pensioners of Thorn and EMI Group.
  • To provide a voice on behalf of all Thorn and EMI Group Pensioners and their dependents, and to represent their interests within the National pension community and specifically through the Thorn and EMI Group Pension Funds.
  • To ensure that, where possible, the Laws of the United Kingdom and of the European Union protect and advance the interests of occupational pension schemes and their pensioners, especially those of the Thorn and EMI Group Pension Funds.

TEPA is an independent organisation with no formal connection to the two Companies.
